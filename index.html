Volleyball!

What is volleyball?

Volleyball is a sport that is played all around the world, although it isn't as famous as football/soccer it's just as fun (maybe even more)! Volleyball is a sport that requires you to have working hands and a good mind set. It might not be as great as I make it out to be - when you first start playing - but trust me, once you get the hang of it and when all of your shots are accurate of course - it'll be SO fun!
